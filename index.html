BID Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name, email address, phone number, age, photos, and profile information such as bio and gender.

2. Location Data

With your permission, we collect location data to show you profiles near you. You can disable this in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the BID service, match you with other users, process payments, and send you notifications about activity on your account.

4. Advertising

BID shows ads to free users. We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Payments

Payments are processed securely through Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not store your payment information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the app, including Supabase for database services.

7. Age Requirement

BID is only available to users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

8. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us. California residents have additional rights under CCPA/CPRA.

9. Data Security

We use industry standard security measures to protect your data. However no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy please contact us at your Gmail address.
